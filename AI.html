Topic brainstorming

With Taiwan's increasing population and growing urbanization, traffic jams have become a daily problem for almost everyone.

Especially during peak hours, traffic not only affects efficiency but also lowers the quality of life and creates unnecessary air pollution.

On the roads, traffic jams are not the only issue troubling us; road safety and exhaust emissions are also challenges modern people must face.

Therefore, we hope to use smart traffic management, such as priority control and traffic light coordination to address these problems.

This will not only ensure the safety of road users but also reduce environmental pollution, helping to make the entire city a better place.



System Introduction

Our operation process has three steps: Data Collection, Data Analysis, and Optimization & Execution.

  1. Data Collection:

    2. Using road monitoring devices and vehicle-to-everything (V2X) technology, we gather real-time information from the roads. Additionally, we collect weather data and combine it with historical data to start the analysis.

  2. Data Analysis:

    3. By analyzing data such as occupancy rate, density, and average delay time, we determine whether a specific road is currently congested or underused. This provides a basis for the road Optimization & Execution.

  3. Optimization & Execution:

    4. There are four main techniques: traffic light coordination, green wave technology, priority control, and road suggestion.
